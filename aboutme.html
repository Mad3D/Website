Sergio Madeira
Senior Technical Artist 

I'm a self-taught 3D artist with an insatiable passion for all things art-related, drawing inspiration from everything around me. With experience working in various industries, including Automotive (Volvo Cars, Geely, Zeekr, Ford, etc.) and Fashion (Converse, Gucci, Mizuno, Balenciaga, Tommy Hilfiger, etc.), I'm driven to constantly learn and improve.

So, if you're looking for a 3D artist who's not afraid to think outside the box and bring your creative vision to life, let's work together! paulo.madeira3d@gmail.com
