Puma Suede

Puma Making of Video

This project was undertaken for Puma as a segment of the Foundry Enterprise initiative. Additionally, I played a key role in providing Modo and Colorway training sessions at Puma's facility in Herzogenaurach, Germany.

Initiating the project with a low-quality photogrammetry base, I leveraged the physical shoe in hand to meticulously replicate real-world imperfections. The scope covered all aspects, including modeling, texturing, lighting, and rendering, executed using Foundry Modo 3D and Substance Painter. The finishing touches were applied with Photoshop.


Shoe heel with stitches and fur. Enhanced with other loving imperfections/details.
