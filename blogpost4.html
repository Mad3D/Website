3D Automotive Material Sample for Download.

This particular sample is widely used by numerous automotive companies as a repository for storing exterior color samples. It's no surprise that studios often have a multitude of physical parts like these on hand.

I created a quick procedural modeling approximation of the shape, allowing for enhanced visualization of the material.

Don't hesitate to click the link below to download the geometry in both OBJ and FBX formats. Feel free to explore and utilize it in your projects!
DOWNLOAD Files 

To ensure flexibility and maintain performance, I opted for a procedural modeling approach for this component. Knowing adjustments to its size were likely, this method allowed for quick and efficient modifications.

For the base, I created a simple rectangular shape and applied procedural thickening and deformation techniques.

Unfortunately, the original procedural file is unavailable, preventing a video demonstration of the process.
