 Voice Recognition 

Controlling with your voice

I’ve always been curious about controlling scenes with my voice, so I experimented with voice recognition on a Control Rig. The plugin I used was a bit hit-or-miss my accent didn’t help—but the process taught me a lot. I’m now much more aware of how to hook voice into Unreal, and I’m excited for the day we can easily link AI like Alexa or Siri to trigger real-time events.

You can find here the Voice Recognition plugin and setup documentation LINK .

As mentioned earlier, this system is more suitable for experimentation than production use. While it’s a great way to explore voice-driven interaction in Unreal, it can be unreliable and prone to misinterpreting commands.

That said, it’s a valuable learning tool, and I believe more robust, AI-driven solutions are on the horizon. There are also paid plugins available that may offer greater accuracy and stability for production workflows.
